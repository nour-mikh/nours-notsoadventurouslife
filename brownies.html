My microwave brownies recipe

Introduction

As you migh have read in the about me section, I love baking! Making microwave recipes is the easiest way of baking so here's my brownies' one !

What's the recipe ?

  • 2 TBSP flour
  • 1.5 TBSP sugar
  • 2 TBSP cocoa (more or less depends on how fudgy you like it)
  • a pinch of salt
  • 1.5 TBSP vegetable oil
  • 2 TBSP water
